Bilbo's Seed-Cake

Description

"But I don't mind some cake â€” seed-cake, if you have any." - Balin.

Seed-Cake straight out of The Hobbit universe made by Bilbo Baggins himself.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1tbsp caraway

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (177 degrees C).

  2. Cream butter and sugar.

  3. Add milk, vanilla, and eggs. Mix well.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour and salt.

  5. Combine dry and wet ingredients.

  6. Mix in caraway.

  7. Pour into prepared baking dish. (To stay true to the book, use a round pan.)

  8. Bake a knife inserted in the center comes out clean (about 50 minutes).
