Grilled Tomahawk Steak

Description

This reverse-seared steak is a showstopper, cooked to perfection with an unctuous compound butter filled with shallots, garlic, and chives. I like to use a Bluetooth-enabled meat thermometer to help me achieve the perfect medium-rare internal temperature.

Ingredients

Steak Rub and Steak

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Montreal steak season
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1(2 3/4 pound) tomahawk steak

Compound Butter

  • 1/4 cup salted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 tbsp minced shallot
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 2 tsp reserved steak rub
